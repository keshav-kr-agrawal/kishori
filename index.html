Our Story

A Bloom That Never Fades

Kishori creation was born from a love of flowers and a desire to make that love last — literally. Every bouquet and pot we create is crafted from carefully sourced dried and preserved flowers that hold their colour, shape, and charm for years.

We believe every home, every shelf, and every moment deserves a touch of botanical beauty. No water needed. No wilting. Just endless spring.
